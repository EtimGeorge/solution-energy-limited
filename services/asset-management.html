Asset Integrity & Management Services

Our Asset Integrity & Management Services focus on ensuring that an asset will perform its desired function over its life cycle. A major problem of great concern for facility owners and AIM engineers is to balance the designing, maintenance and replacement of assets throughout their life cycle, while minimising costs to business in terms of finance, time and resources.

Our Solutions Mitigate These Challenges

  • Corrosion Monitoring
  • Corrosion Prevention
  • Automated Corrosion Mapping
  • Cathodic Protection Services
  • Inspection Services (Automated, Statutory, Voluntary etc.)
  • Non-Destructive Testing
  • Remaining-Life Assessment
  • Material Testing
  • Regular monitoring of activities
  • Calibration Services (flowmeter, Road truck, Prover tank, Shore Tank, Storage Tank etc.)

Facility Maintenance Services

Our Service sector which covers the energy sector in general portends high risk and requires maximum safety standards, hence we have built expertise to deliver top-notch maintenance services with state-of-the-art equipment, some semi or fully automated.

Our Maintenance Services Include:

  • Overhaul, repair & recertification of mechanical and process modules
  • Sectional or entire pipeline or piping replacement
  • Electrical & instrumentation maintenance & installation
  • Coating/painting & surface protection services
  • Routine lubrication of all moving parts
  • Replacement of valves, pumps etc.
  • Regular inspections of lighting systems
  • Scaffolding and Rope Access Services
